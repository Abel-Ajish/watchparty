Alpha Development Phase

Thanks for trying out this app... This app is still under development. Expect it to be really rough!

1. Sync Stuttering: Some stuttering while syncing is normal. Please give it time to stabilize.
2. Mobile Playback: Some YouTube video's (especially music video's) don't work on mobile because the owner disabled embedding. I am still looking for a workaround.
3. Moving to next video: If the next video is not playing, move the video slider (the playing video on screen) towards the end to force the next one.
4. Missing in Queue: If a video doesn't show in the queue, try reloading the website.
